Nodd Pro-V

NODD PRO-V (Fractional RF Technology) allows effective treatment of the deeper dermis, epidermis, and upper dermis. RF energy is emitted at the end of the fractional RF micro-needle; this will treat the target area – deeper dermis – without damage to the epidermis. The fractional RF is useful for minimal invasive epidermal rejuvenation. RF energy does not target pigment-making cells in the skin. NODD cures and improves wrinkles, scars, Skin rejuvenation, and skin tightening safely and efficiently with minimized PIH, using RF energy.

  • Microneedle RF is for invasive treatment.
  • An effective and direct thermal stimulation for remodeling collagen with less damage on the epidermis.
  • Stepping motor-driven mechanism enables to control of penetration depth precisely and accurately.
  • Micro needle delivers RF directly to target area and reforms collagen and elastin effectively without any energy loss like laser treatment.
  • Microneedle RF penetrates target area from epidermis to dermis without energy loss and stimulates disordered collagen in order to remodel it efficiently.

Technology

NODD Fractional RF Microneedle is designed specially for delivery RF energy directly to epidermis or dermis layer in order to stimulate and re-model collagen effectively by either RF micro-needling mode(invasive) or RF matrix mode(non-invasive). It can be used for both epidermal application and dermal applications in one platform without change any operational environment.

The Newest Generation In Advance Microneedling

Microneedling Fractional Rf system is an ideal technology for skin lifting, tightening and rejuvenation by applying precisely controlled RF energy directly into various depths of skin with minimally invasive micro-needles. It can be applied to all skin types, with very low risk of skin burns and PIH. This is a key difference when compared to optical based treatments (e.g. IPL/Laser).

ADVANTAGE-VACUUM

NODD revolutionizes RF microneedling with a dual air chamber design, capturing air through enhanced tissue engagement. Microneedles create channels, delivering RF energy and releasing air upon retraction, driving topicals up to 67% deeper into the dermis with M and F Type needles. Versatile and customizable, NODD takes microneedling to new levels for all skin types and body areas, year-round.
