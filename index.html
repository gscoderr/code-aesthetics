Rental Solutions That Help Grow Your Business

Renting laser equipment is the perfect way to offer treatments and add revenue without the out of pocket cost or risk of laser ownership. Rental gives you the opportunity to offer your clients an entire laser center without the purchase of a single laser! There are hundreds of lasers flooding the market and with machinery moving forward, new technologies are being discovered and lasers are updated frequently. Why purchase one of today's industry lasers for thousands of dollars, when it will most likely be outdated tomorrow? To avoid financial risk, the majority of medical centers are renting mobile laser devices. Here are a few more advantages of renting rather than purchasing this equipment:

Training, Service & Maintenance

We understand that you may have limited knowledge of how your laser functions, or limited experience operating these devices. Laser Service Solutions will provide full training upon delivery of your system and will always be available to answer questions throughout the term of the lease. Renting our state of the art technology means you can always be sure that you're getting the most recent product on the market. We keep up with the popular demand of the ever-changing technologies in the medical field by finding ways to better our product and offer the newest lasers in the industry.

Space Saving

Owning laser equipment is your responsibility to find a space to store it when it is not in use. By renting our equipment, you can have the laser on hand when needed, and you can send it back with the use is over and you do not have to worry about finding the space to store it.

No Financial Risk

Avoid paying thousands out of pocket for each individual laser that will most likely be outdated in the near future anyway! renting lasers gives you the freedom to trade up and always have the newest technology for your facility.
